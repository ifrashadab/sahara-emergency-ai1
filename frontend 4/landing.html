Under the hood

Retrieval-Augmented Generation, not guesswork.

Generic chatbots improvise medical advice. SAHARA doesn't. Every query is embedded and semantically matched against a curated knowledge base built from NHS, Red Cross, and St John Ambulance guidance. The LLM answers only from what was retrieved — and every response includes an expandable panel showing the exact sources, the retrieval pipeline, and the severity reasoning.

Questions

FAQ

Is this a replacement for calling emergency services?

No — and it never claims to be. For critical situations SAHARA's first instruction is always to contact emergency services. It exists for the minutes before help arrives, and for situations where formal services aren't the right first step.

Where does the AI's advice come from?

From a curated knowledge base built on published guidance from the NHS, Red Cross, and St John Ambulance. The AI is instructed to answer only from retrieved entries — and to say so when nothing relevant is found, rather than improvising.

Who are the volunteers?

Community members who register on the platform. Requests are matched by proximity and help type, and volunteers build a track record through the karma and leaderboard system.

What happens to my data?

Help requests are stored to coordinate your case with volunteers. Location is shared only when you explicitly share it during a request.
